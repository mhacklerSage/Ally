What type of business do you have?

    A physical or online store where you sell items. Your customers make purchases in-person or via a shopping cart on your web page.

  • Restaurant

    Restaurant

    A physical restaurant, where customers sit to eat then pay their bill in-person with cash or credit cards.

    Services

    You provide services to customers at various locations. Customers pay immediately, in-person or may be sent an invoice.
